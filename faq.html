About Us FAQ

Common Questions.

Why not just use standard WhatsApp Business?

WhatsApp Business is great for chatting, but it doesn't do accounting. It can't create tax invoices, track inventory levels, or generate reports. We connect WhatsApp to Zoho so you get the sales AND the paperwork done automatically.

How long does setup take?

We move fast. The Digital Storefront usually takes 5-7 days. The full Automation Core takes about 10-14 days, including a testing phase to ensure your invoices are 100% accurate.

What do you need from me to start?

Very little. We primarily need your Product List (Excel/CSV), your Logo, and access to a dedicated phone number for the bot. We handle the rest of the technical setup.

Are there monthly fees?

RJ Digital charges a one-time setup fee. However, you may pay small direct subscription fees to Zoho (approx R150/pm) or WhatsApp API (usage based) depending on your volume. We do not mark these up.

Can I use Sage or Xero?

Our standard "Automation Core" package is built on Zoho Invoice because it is cost-effective and reliable. However, we can integrate with Sage or Xero as a Custom Enterprise project. Contact us for a quote.

What happens if WhatsApp goes down?

Because we use the official Meta Cloud API, reliability is very high. If WhatsApp goes down, your data remains safe in your database, and the system automatically resumes processing orders once the connection is restored.

Can I update the catalog myself?

Yes. You own the accounts. We provide a training video on how to add or remove products from your WhatsApp Catalog instantly.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. We act as a POPIA "Operator". Your data lives in your own secure Google Sheets and Zoho account. We just build the secure pipes that move the data.
