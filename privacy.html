Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Ryden Jonathan T/A RJ Digital Systems ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we process personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

We operate in two capacities:

  • As a Responsible Party: When you visit our website or inquire about our services.
  • As an Operator: When we build automation systems that process your customers' data on your behalf.

2. Information We Collect

From Clients (You)

We collect your Name, Business Name, WhatsApp Number, Email Address, and Billing Details solely for the purpose of service delivery and invoicing.

From Your Customers (Via Automation)

When you hire us to build a system, our automation scripts (Make.com/Zoho) may technically process your customers' Names, Phone Numbers, and Order Histories. We do not store this data for our own use. It is passed directly to your own private accounts (Google Sheets/Zoho).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To communicate with you via WhatsApp regarding your system audit or project.
  • To issue invoices and process payments via Yoco.
  • To configure your automation environment (e.g., setting up your Zoho Invoice account).

4. Third-Party Sub-Processors

To provide our "Hybrid Automation" services, we utilize world-class infrastructure. By using our services, you consent to data passing through:

Google Cloud Database hosting (Sheets/Drive).
Meta (WhatsApp) Messaging infrastructure.
Make.com (Celonis) Automation logic processing.
Zoho Corporation Invoicing and CRM.

5. Security & Retention

We employ a "Least Privilege" architecture. We only request access to the specific folders and accounts needed to build your system.

Once a project is handed over, you (the Client) act as the administrator. We retain your contact details for support purposes for as long as you remain a client, or for 5 years as required by South African Tax Law (SARS).

6. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing for marketing purposes.
